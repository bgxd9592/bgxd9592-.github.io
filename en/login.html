foreword

In order to allow players to customize their own skins and see each other's skins, we have prepared a skin station for the server. At the same time, in order to make login more convenient, we have set up an external login - that is, log in before starting the game, and log in after Startup, based on authlib-injector, allows players to log in to the game more conveniently without having to enter /reg, /l, etc. to log in again after entering the game.

Since genuine users can directly set their own skins, YggdrasilOfficialProxy is used to make genuine login and external login coexist.

Therefore, we strongly request genuine users to bind their genuine accounts and IDs on the skin site to prevent some external login players from stealing IDs.
